Lasagna

Description

Some recipes take a little more time to make but are so worth the effort — like lasagna. This versatile Italian dish can be labor-intensive, but the prep is worth it for the delicious final product. (It's no wonder Garfield the cat loved them so much.) Allrecipes has hundreds of lasagna recipes to choose from, but we've rounded up our 15 best lasagna recipes of all time. Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • ½ cup water
